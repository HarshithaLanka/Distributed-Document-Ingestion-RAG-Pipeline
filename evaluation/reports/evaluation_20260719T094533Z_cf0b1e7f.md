**Answer:**

Encapsulation in RTOS means dividing the system into separate tasks or modules, where each task handles one well-defined function and hides its internal working from the others. Other tasks interact only through proper RTOS mechanisms such as semaphores, queues, or message passing, instead of directly changing each other's internal data.
